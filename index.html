Atlas TravelPay

International prepaid debit cards for outbound travelers. Issued by licensed partners. Designed with compliance first.

How It Works

1. Verify

Register at an authorized service desk or online. Identity verification is required in accordance with AML and KYC regulations.

2. Load Funds

Funds are loaded through approved payment channels and safeguarded by a licensed electronic money issuer.

3. Use Abroad

Spend internationally at supported merchants, payment terminals, and ATMs, subject to network acceptance.

Security & Regulation

Atlas TravelPay operates as a service and onboarding provider. Customer funds are held exclusively by licensed issuing partners. Atlas TravelPay does not accept deposits, provide banking services, or hold customer balances.

All services are subject to identity verification, transaction monitoring, sanctions screening, and applicable laws.

Legal & Compliance Notices

  • Terms & Conditions: Use of the service is subject to contractual terms provided at onboarding.
  • Privacy Policy: Personal data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
  • AML / KYC: Identity verification is mandatory. Transactions may be declined or reported.
  • Sanctions: Service is unavailable to sanctioned individuals or prohibited jurisdictions.
